My idea is based on Slavoj Zizek's thoughts on sexual fantasies, and how the worst thing that can happen to one is if it's comes to life. Together with that he always mentioned an anecdote which describes an ultimate date scenario, in which he asks a woman out, and when they come home together, she takes out a dildo, he takes out an electronic vagina, and the toys have sex for them while the couple continues enjoying the dinner. I wrote a text of two parts reenacting this situation.
